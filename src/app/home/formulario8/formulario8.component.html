Hazardous Items Search Consent

Upon acceptance into Time For Change Recovery House all resident’s property will be searched by a Time For Change Recovery House staff member. Prior to searching staff will ask resident if there is anything in the resident’s belongings that may harm the staff member while searching. Any items found that may be deemed harmful to staff or residents will not be permitted on Time For Change Recovery House property. Any drugs or paraphanelia found will be confiscated and disposed of according to local code and ordance of Broward County/State of Florida. Weapons of any sort will not be allowed on property by any resident. This includes but is not limited to guns, knives, swords, or explosives and will be turned over to local law enforcement (this includes individuals who have permits to carry weapons from the state of Florida.) Time For Change Recovery House will adhere to the standards set forth by the state probation office or prison system to protect residents while living at Time For Change Recovery House. I also understand that at anytime during my stay, my personal belongings may be searched and if any item is found after my initial search upon arrival of the program, I may be terminated from the Time For Change Recovery House Program.
