DISCHARGE POLICY & PROCEDURE

1. The resident will provide two weeks’ notice prior to departure. 2. All furnishings undamaged and no items missing. 3. All personnel belongings removed from the residence at discharge/move out. 4. If two weeks’ notice is provided, the security deposit will be returned to the resident upon departure - Discharge will be maintained in clients file, and necessary referrals for continuing with 12 step meetings will be provided. 5. Client will have 24 hours to have all personal effects removed from property.

ASA Discharge: The resident leaves against the advice of Staff.

1. If two weeks’ notice is not provided, the security deposit will not be returned. 2. If belongings are left without prior arrangements, the belongings are stored for 7 days; at the end of the 7 days, belongings are donated to a non-profit thrift store and documented in the resident’s file. 3. If resident’s medications are left at the residence, the medications will be disposed of following discharge (see Page 38 for Disposal of Medications). 4. The combo locks at the residents will be changed after the resident’s departure. 5. Emergency contact will be notified, and discharge will be documented in file. 6. Client will have 1 hour to pack belongings and vacate the property.

Abandonment Discharge: The resident leaves property without notice and/or without personal property.

1. The security deposit will not be returned. 2. Abandoned belongings are stored for 7 days; at the end of the 7 days, belongings are donated to a non-profit thrift store and documented in the resident’s file. 3. If resident’s medications are left at the residence, the medications will be disposed of following discharge (see Page 38 for Disposal of Medications). 4. The combo locks at the residents will be changed after the resident’s departure. 5. Emergency contact will be notified, and discharge will be documented in file. 6. Clients belongings will be packed by house man or manager within 1 hour of discharge.
